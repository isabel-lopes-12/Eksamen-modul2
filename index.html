Find your path. Shape your Future.

EduPath helps you evaluate career paths using real labour-market data, long-term trends, and forward projections — in Norway and globally. Compare professions based on salary development, job growth, transition mobility, economic output, and future competitiveness.

"The best way to predict the future is to create it."

Every career has a story shaped by decades of technological, economic, and social change. From the rise of the smartphone to the era of social media, electric vehicles, and AI innovations, historical shifts reveal patterns that guide the future.

EduPath lets you explore careers with insight: employment trends, salary development, global competitiveness, and pivotal moments that have transformed professions. Make informed choices that consider not just what interests you today, but what will thrive tomorrow.

About EduPath


EduPath is a collaborative education platform developed in partnership with


Universitetet i Oslo,
Norges teknisk-naturvitenskapelige universitet,
Universitetet i Bergen, and
Universitetet i Tromsø – Norges arktiske universitet.


The platform provides structured, comparable information about academic degrees, professional studies, and vocational pathways to support informed educational and career decisions. Our goal is to improve transparency, accessibility, and informed choice across Norwegian education.
